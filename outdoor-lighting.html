Outdoor
Lighting

Layered low-voltage lighting that extends your evenings outside and highlights the landscape after dark.

Overview

Your landscape doesn't clock out at sunset

Good outdoor lighting is layered, not flooded — path lights for safety, uplighting on specimen trees and architectural features, and soft ambient glow on patios and seating areas. We design each layer separately so you can run them independently and never light your whole yard like a parking lot just to walk to the mailbox.

All fixtures are low-voltage, energy-efficient, and controllable from your phone, with smart timers that adjust automatically as sunset times shift through the year.

What's included

  • Path, step, and safety lighting
  • Architectural uplighting and tree/canopy lighting
  • Smart transformers, timers, and app control
  • Annual bulb and fixture maintenance plans available

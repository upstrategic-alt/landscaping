Landscape
Design

Master-planned outdoor spaces that work with your land's natural grade, light, and drainage — not a catalog template forced onto your lot.

Overview

A plan before a single shovel hits the ground

Every Stonebrook design begins on foot, not on paper. Our designers walk your property at different times of day to understand sun exposure, drainage patterns, and the sightlines that matter most from inside your home. Only then do we draft a master plan that balances plantings, hardscape, and open space.

You'll receive a detailed 2D plan (3D renderings available for full-property redesigns), a curated plant and material palette suited to Central Texas's climate, and a phased budget so you can build now or over several seasons without losing design cohesion.

What's included

  • On-site analysis of soil, drainage, and sun exposure
  • Custom master plan with plant and material palette
  • Phased budget options for multi-season builds
  • One revision round included before final sign-off

Terms of
Service

Last updated June 2026

Estimates & Agreements

All on-site consultations and design quotes from Stonebrook Landscape Co. are free and non-binding. Work begins only after both parties sign a written project agreement outlining scope, materials, timeline, and price. Estimates are valid for 30 days unless otherwise stated.

Change Orders

Any change to the agreed scope of work — additional materials, design changes, or site conditions discovered after excavation begins — will be documented in a written change order and approved by the client before additional work proceeds.

Payment Terms

Standard payment terms are a deposit at signing, a progress payment at the midpoint of larger projects, and final payment due upon completion and client walkthrough sign-off. Specific terms are set out in each project agreement.

Warranty

Stonebrook warranties workmanship on hardscape and structural installations for two years from completion. Plant material is warrantied for 90 days, contingent on the client following the provided care and irrigation schedule. Manufacturer warranties apply separately to fixtures, controllers, and equipment.

Limitation of Liability

Stonebrook is not liable for pre-existing site conditions not disclosed prior to contract signing, including unmarked utility lines, undisclosed drainage easements, or soil contamination.

Contact

Questions about these terms can be directed to hello@stonebrooklandscape.com or (512) 555-0148.

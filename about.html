About Stonebrook

Fifteen Years
of Rooted Work

Stonebrook started as a two-person design-build crew in 2011. Today we're a full design, hardscape, and maintenance team — still showing up to every walkthrough ourselves.

Our Story

One crew, every trade

Most landscape companies subcontract hardscape, irrigation, and lighting to separate crews who've never met each other, let alone seen your design plan. Stonebrook does it differently: the same in-house team that draws your master plan also pours your patio, sets your irrigation lines, and wires your lighting — so nothing gets lost in translation between trades.

We're licensed, bonded, and insured in the state of Texas, and every crew lead has been with Stonebrook for at least four years. That continuity is why our repeat and referral business now makes up most of our annual workload.

Why property owners choose us

  • One accountable crew from design through final walkthrough
  • Native and drought-tolerant plant expertise specific to Central Texas
  • Transparent, itemized estimates — no change-order surprises
  • Maintenance programs available for everything we build

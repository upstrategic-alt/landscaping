Policy

Information We Collect

When you request a quote, call, or email Stonebrook Landscape Co., we collect the information you provide directly — your name, phone number, email address, property address, and project details. We do not collect information through this website beyond what you submit voluntarily; this site does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your inquiry, schedule consultations, prepare estimates, and deliver the services you request. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

We retain client information for as long as needed to support ongoing service, maintenance programs, and warranty obligations, and as required by applicable Texas business recordkeeping requirements.

Your Choices

You may request that we delete your contact information from our records at any time by emailing hello@stonebrooklandscape.com, except where we are required to retain records for legal or warranty purposes.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to hello@stonebrooklandscape.com or (512) 555-0148.

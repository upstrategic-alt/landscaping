Common Questions

Does financing affect the design process?

No. Every project — financed, phased, or paid in full — starts with the same on-site consultation and master plan. We'll talk through budget early so the design reflects what's realistic for your timeline.

Is there a minimum project size to finance?

Most lending partners require a minimum project total of $5,000. Smaller projects can still be phased without financing.

How long does approval take?

Most homeowners receive a financing decision within minutes of applying through our lending partner, with no impact on credit score for a soft pre-qualification check.

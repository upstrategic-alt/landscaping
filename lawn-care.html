Lawn Care &

Seasonal mowing, fertilization, and bed care programs that keep your investment looking sharp year-round.

Overview

The design only looks good if it's maintained

A beautifully designed landscape that's poorly maintained fades fast — and a maintenance crew that didn't build the space often doesn't understand the design intent. Stonebrook maintenance clients get the same crews who do our installs, so beds get trimmed, not butchered, and turf gets treated for Austin's specific soil and climate conditions.

Plans are available whether or not we built your landscape originally — we'll do an initial site assessment either way.

What's included

  • Weekly or biweekly mowing, edging, and blowing
  • Seasonal fertilization and targeted weed control
  • Bed maintenance, pruning, and mulch refresh
  • Aeration and overseeding programs each fall
